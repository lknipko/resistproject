## Sustained Actions

### Donate to Abortion Funds

Abortion funds provide financial assistance, travel support, lodging, childcare, and logistical help to people seeking abortion care. Your donation directly enables someone to access care they couldn't otherwise afford.

#### National Networks

- **[National Network of Abortion Funds (NNAF)](https://abortionfunds.org/)** - Network of nearly 100 grassroots abortion funds across the U.S. Donate to the national fund (split among all funds) or choose specific state/regional funds.

- **[National Abortion Federation (NAF) Hotline Fund](https://prochoice.org/donate/)** - Provides financial assistance directly to abortion providers to cover patient costs. Also operates a hotline connecting callers with providers.

- **[Women's Reproductive Rights Assistance Project (WRRAP)](https://wrrap.org/)** - Helps bridge the financial gap for people seeking abortion or emergency contraceptives.

#### Regional Abortion Funds

**Midwest:**
- **[Chicago Abortion Fund](https://chicagoabortionfund.org/)** - Financial, emotional, and logistical support for Illinois residents
- **[Midwest Access Coalition](https://midwestaccesscoalition.org/)** - Travel, accommodations, food, childcare, and medicine for people traveling to the Midwest for abortion care

**South:**
- **[Texas Equal Access Fund](https://www.teafund.org/)** - Financial assistance for Texans traveling out of state for abortion care
- **[New River Abortion Access Fund](https://www.newriverabortionfund.org/)** - Serves Virginia, West Virginia, Kentucky, Tennessee

**West:**
- **[Northwest Abortion Access Fund](https://nwaafund.org/)** - Serves Washington, Oregon, Idaho, Alaska

**East:**
- Many state-based funds serve the Northeast and Mid-Atlantic regions

**Find your local abortion fund:** [NNAF Fund Finder](https://abortionfunds.org/need-abortion/)

### Join Advocacy Organizations

These organizations coordinate legal challenges, policy advocacy, and grassroots organizing to protect reproductive rights:

#### National Organizations

**[Planned Parenthood Action Fund](https://www.plannedparenthoodaction.org/)**
- Advocacy and political arm of Planned Parenthood
- Lobbies Congress, files lawsuits, mobilizes voters
- **How to join:** Become a member, sign up for action alerts, attend local events

**[Reproductive Freedom for All](https://reproductivefreedomforall.org/)** (formerly NARAL Pro-Choice America)
- 4 million members organizing for reproductive freedom
- Holds lawmakers accountable, coordinates state ballot measures
- **How to join:** [Join at their website](https://reproductivefreedomforall.org/)

**[Center for Reproductive Rights](https://reproductiverights.org/)**
- Legal organization filing cases in courts and before human rights bodies
- Successfully challenged Missouri's abortion ban regulations in 2026
- **How to join:** Donate, sign up for updates, support litigation

**[ACLU Reproductive Freedom Project](https://www.aclu.org/issues/reproductive-freedom)**
- Coordinates multi-state litigation challenging abortion restrictions
- Filed lawsuits in Arkansas, Missouri, and other states in 2025-2026
- **How to join:** Become an ACLU member, volunteer with your state ACLU chapter

**[National Organization for Women (NOW)](https://now.org/issues/abortion-rights-reproductive-issues/)**
- Feminist organization advocating for abortion rights codification
- Encourages contacting Capitol Switchboard: 202-224-3121
- **How to join:** Join your local NOW chapter

**[Congressional Reproductive Freedom Caucus](https://reproductivefreedom.house.gov/)**
- 160+ House members committed to advancing reproductive rights
- Co-Chairs: Rep. Diana DeGette (CO) and Rep. Ayanna Pressley (MA)
- **How to support:** Contact your representative to urge them to join

### Support Litigation Efforts

Legal challenges are the frontline defense against abortion restrictions. These organizations are actively suing to block bans and protect access:

#### Current Major Cases (2026)

**Missouri Abortion Regulations Case:**
- **Who's suing:** Planned Parenthood, ACLU of Missouri, Center for Reproductive Rights
- **What's at stake:** Whether Missouri's abortion regulations violate the state's new reproductive rights amendment
- **Status:** Trial began January 2026; victory would restore full abortion access in Missouri
- **How to support:** Donate to the organizations involved

[Missouri Independent - Trial Coverage](https://missouriindependent.com/2026/01/09/missouri-abortion-regulations-trial-access-trap-laws/)

**Mifepristone FDA Approval Challenges:**
- **Who's suing:** Louisiana, Texas, Florida (challenging FDA)
- **Who's defending:** DOJ, with support from Center for Reproductive Rights and ACLU
- **What's at stake:** Medication abortion access nationwide
- **Status:** Multiple lawsuits pending in 2026
- **How to support:** Contact DOJ to urge vigorous defense, donate to intervener organizations

**Arkansas Abortion Restrictions:**
- **Who's suing:** Center for Reproductive Rights, ACLU, ACLU of Arkansas
- **What's at stake:** Four abortion restrictions passed in 2025
- **Status:** Lawsuit filed 2025, ongoing
- **How to support:** Donate to the organizations involved

[Center for Reproductive Rights - Arkansas Case](https://reproductiverights.org/news/center-for-reproductive-rights-aclu-file-new-lawsuit-challenging-four-abortion-restrictions-passed-in-arkansas-this-year/)

#### How to Support Litigation

1. **Donate to litigation funds:**
   - [Center for Reproductive Rights - Donate](https://reproductiverights.org/donate/)
   - [ACLU - Support Reproductive Freedom](https://www.aclu.org/give/reproductive-freedom)
   - [Planned Parenthood Action Fund - Donate](https://www.plannedparenthoodaction.org/donate)

2. **Sign amicus briefs** (friend-of-the-court briefs) when organizations recruit signatures

3. **Attend rallies** supporting reproductive rights litigation

### Protect Medication Abortion

**Why this matters:** Medication abortion (mifepristone + misoprostol) accounts for 60% of all U.S. abortions. The FDA has delayed a safety review until after the 2026 midterms, but access remains under threat from lawsuits and potential Comstock Act enforcement.

**Actions:**

1. **Contact the FDA:**
   - Email: [FDA Contact Form](https://www.fda.gov/about-fda/contact-fda)
   - Urge them to maintain current telehealth and mail-order access
   - Oppose any restrictions not based on scientific evidence

2. **Support State Shield Laws:**
   - Advocate for state legislation protecting providers who mail abortion pills across state lines
   - Contact your state legislators to support "shield law" bills

3. **Know Your Rights:**
   - Medication abortion remains legal via telehealth in many states
   - Resources: [Plan C Pills](https://www.plancpills.org/) (information on accessing abortion pills)
   - [Aid Access](https://aidaccess.org/) (international telemedicine service)

4. **Oppose Comstock Act Enforcement:**
   - Call your representatives to oppose DOJ enforcement of Comstock
   - Support federal legislation to repeal the Comstock Act entirely

5. **Vote in 2026 Midterms:**
   - The FDA delayed its mifepristone review until after the November 2026 midterms
   - Congressional control will determine whether abortion restrictions can be blocked
   - **Register to vote:** [Vote.gov](https://vote.gov)
   - Check your registration and request absentee ballot if needed
