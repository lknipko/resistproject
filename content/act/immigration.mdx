## Sustained Actions

### Support Legal Defense Organizations

These organizations provide direct legal representation to immigrants:

| Organization | Focus Area | How to Help |
|--------------|------------|-------------|
| [RAICES](https://www.raicestexas.org/) | Texas border legal services | Donate, volunteer |
| [Florence Project](https://firrp.org/) | Arizona detention legal aid | Donate, pro bono attorneys |
| [CLINIC](https://cliniclegal.org/) | National legal network | Find local affiliate |
| [ACLU Immigrants' Rights](https://www.aclu.org/issues/immigrants-rights) | Litigation and advocacy | Donate, take action alerts |
| [National Immigration Law Center](https://www.nilc.org/) | Policy and litigation | Donate, advocacy updates |

### Join Local Rapid Response Networks

Rapid response networks mobilize community members when immigration enforcement occurs:

- **United We Dream - Local Chapters:** [Find your local hub](https://unitedwedream.org/our-work/)
- **Immigrant Defense Project:** [ICE raid response toolkit](https://www.immigrantdefenseproject.org/)
- **Witness at the Border:** [Document and bear witness](https://witnessattheborder.org/)

**What Rapid Responders Do:**
- Observe and document enforcement actions (from public spaces)
- Connect families with legal resources
- Provide translation and accompaniment services
- Alert community networks

### Become a Legal Observer or Accompaniment Volunteer

- **ACLU Legal Observer Training:** [Find local trainings](https://www.aclu.org/issues/free-speech/rights-protesters)
- **Accompaniment Programs:** Walk with community members to court dates, ICE check-ins
- **Court Watch Programs:** Monitor immigration court proceedings for due process violations

### Support Immigrant-Led Organizations

- [United We Dream](https://unitedwedream.org/) - Largest immigrant youth-led network
- [Make the Road New York](https://maketheroadny.org/) - Grassroots community organizing
- [CASA](https://wearecasa.org/) - Mid-Atlantic immigrant advocacy
- [Coalition for Humane Immigrant Rights (CHIRLA)](https://www.chirla.org/) - California-based organizing

### [+] If You Are An Employer

- [I-9 Audit Preparation Guide](https://www.nilc.org/issues/workersrights/) - Know your obligations and rights
- You can require a judicial warrant (signed by a judge) before allowing ICE access to non-public workspaces
- Document any enforcement interactions

### [+] If You Are A Landlord or Property Owner

- ICE generally requires a judicial warrant to enter private property
- [Know the difference](https://www.ilrc.org/warrants) between administrative warrants (signed by ICE agent) and judicial warrants (signed by judge)
