## Why This Matters

**Constitutional Crisis:**
- Congress has exclusive authority over spending (Article I)
- DOGE bypassing legislative branch entirely
- Separation of powers under attack

**Service Disruptions:**
- Social Security delays affecting retirees
- Veterans' benefits processing slowed
- Food safety inspections reduced
- Weather forecasting capabilities threatened

**Conflicts of Interest:**
- Elon Musk has $15B in government contracts
- SpaceX, Tesla benefit from federal programs
- No recusal or transparency measures

**Economic Impact:**
- 260,000 jobs lost
- Local economies dependent on federal facilities
- Private contractors replacing civil servants at higher cost

**Your Voice Matters:**
- Congressional oversight is the constitutional check
- Constituent pressure drives congressional action
- Transparency requires public demand
