## Multiple Topics (Alternative Pattern)

If you have multiple related messages on the same topic, use the compound component pattern with a topic selector:

### Call Your Representatives - Multiple Topics

<CallRepButton repType="all">
  <CallScript
    topic="Topic 1 Name"
    script={`Hello, I'm {firstName} {lastName} from {zipCode}.

I'm calling to urge {repName} to [specific ask for topic 1].

Thank you.`}
  />
  <CallScript
    topic="Topic 2 Name"
    script={`Hi, my name is {firstName} {lastName} and I'm a constituent from {zipCode}.

I'm calling about [topic 2 issue]...

Thank you.`}
  />
</CallRepButton>

### Email Your Representatives - Multiple Topics

<EmailTemplate repType="all">
  <EmailMessage
    topic="Topic 1 Name"
    subject="Subject for topic 1"
    body={`Dear {repName},

I am writing from {zipCode} regarding [topic 1]...

Sincerely,
{firstName} {lastName}`}
  />
  <EmailMessage
    topic="Topic 2 Name"
    subject="Subject for topic 2"
    body={`Dear {repName},

I am writing as your constituent from {zipCode} about [topic 2]...

Sincerely,
{firstName} {lastName}`}
  />
</EmailTemplate>
